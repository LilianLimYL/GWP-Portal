➕ How to Add a New Shipment
🔄 This portal reads live from your Google Sheet. To add a shipment:
1.Open your GWP Master Tracker Google Sheet
2.Go to the GWP Tracker tab at the bottom
3.Add a new row at the bottom with all the shipment details
4.Fill in: Job No, Product Name, Handler, Handler Email 1, Status, ETA Date, Freight Mode, Latest Update
5.Save the sheet → come back here → click 🔄 Refresh live data
6.The new shipment appears automatically. Brand owners see it within 60 seconds.

✏️ How to Update Shipment Status
📊 Daily update routine — takes 30 seconds per shipment
1.Open GWP Master Tracker in Google Sheets
2.Find the shipment row by Job No or Product Name
3.Update the Status column (Open / Completed / Pending / Delayed)
4.Update the Latest Update column — paste your new status note with date prefix: 16 May 26 - Arrived at Packxpert
5.Update the ETA Date column if it changed
6.Save — the portal auto-refreshes within 60 seconds. No GitHub needed.
Status values to use in the sheet
Open = Active in progress Completed = Arrived Pending = Awaiting action Delayed = Behind schedule
👥 Managing Brand Owner Access
🔐 How brand owner access works
•Each brand owner logs in by selecting their name. They see only shipments where their email appears in the Handler Email columns.
•To give a new brand owner access: add their email to the Handler Email 1 or Handler Email 2 column in the relevant shipment rows in Google Sheets.
•To add them to the login screen: add their details to the USERS array in the HTML file (one-time setup per new person).
•To remove access: remove their email from the shipment rows. They will see no shipments when they log in.
