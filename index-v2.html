👥 Managing Brand Owner Access
🔐 How brand owner access works
•Each brand owner logs in by selecting their name. They see only shipments where their email appears in the Handler Email columns.
•To give a new brand owner access: add their email to the Handler Email 1 or Handler Email 2 column in the relevant shipment rows in Google Sheets.
•To add them to the login screen: add their details to the USERS array in the HTML file (one-time setup per new person).
•To remove access: remove their email from the shipment rows. They will see no shipments when they log in.
